Flow Mapper South America Banana Trade Flows

Looking at the map, the flows start from northern areas on the continent, and are received by countries that are farther south. The countries with the highest number of bananas according to the nodes are Argentina and Ecuador. This is interesting, since Ecuador is a lot farther north than Argentina is. The choropleth base map indicates that the southern countries are gaining bananas, while the countries farther north are exporting them to those southern countries. After analyzing the nodes, flows, and choropleth basemap, it is easy to tell that the main pattern of the banana trade is that they are moving from north to south within the continent.

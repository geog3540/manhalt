Megan Anhalt Student

I am a U2G student at the University of Iowa, currently completing my Bachelor’s of Arts in Geography while pursuing a Master’s of Science in Urban and Regional Planning with a concentration in transportation. Through my coursework, I have gained a deep understanding about our world and how humans interact with it and am eager to become a planner focused on sustainability and equity.

My primary interest lies in combining sustainability, transportation and GIS to create transportation systems that are safe, equitable, and environmentally responsible. I have hands-on experience working with GIS both academically and in a professional office environment. I am proficient in ArcMap, ArcGIS Pro, and ENVI.
